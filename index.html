आङ्गिकं भुवनं यस्य वाचिकं सर्ववाङ्मयम् ।
आहार्यं चन्द्रतारादि तं वन्दे सात्त्विकं शिवम् ॥

Āṅgikaṃ bhuvanaṃ yasya vācikaṃ sarva-vāṅmayam
āhāryaṃ candra-tārādi taṃ vande sāttvikaṃ śivam

“We bow to Lord Shiva, the embodiment of grace — whose body is the universe, whose speech is all language, whose adornments are the moon and the stars.”

With hearts full of joy and gratitude, we invite you to witness the Ranga Pravesha of Aadhya and Tanvi — the culmination of years of devotion to the art of Bharatanatyam. On this sacred day, they take the stage to offer the fruits of their training to their guru, their families, and the divine. Your presence and blessings would mean the world to us.

Under the guidance of

Guru Sheela Chandrashekar

Founder & Director · Rathi School of Dance

Trained under the loving guidance of Guru Sheela Chandrashekar and the Rathi Arts Foundation, Aadhya and Tanvi carry forward a cherished parampara — a living lineage passed from guru to disciple across generations.
