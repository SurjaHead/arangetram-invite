Artist

Aadhya Sure

Daughter of Ram Mohan Sure and Vasanthi Pasam, presenting her Bharatanatyam Ranga Pravesha under the guidance of Guru Sheela Chandrashekar.

Artist

Thanvi Vashishta Nagineni

Daughter of Ramana Nagineni and Swapna Pachava, presenting her Bharatanatyam Ranga Pravesha under the guidance of Guru Sheela Chandrashekar.

Musicians

The guru and live ensemble bringing the Ranga Pravesha to life.

Nattuvangam

Guru Sheela Chandrashekar

Conducting and cymbals, guiding the rhythm and structure of the performance.

Vocal

Sri. Elangovan Govindarajan

Lead singer, carrying the compositions and devotional storytelling.

Mridangam

Sri. Raghavan Thuraisingamathan

Classical percussion, in rhythmic conversation with the dancers' footwork.

Flute

Sri. Raghuraman Govindarajan

Melodic accompaniment, adding a lyrical Carnatic voice to the concert.

Supporting Team

  • MC: Shalini Chandan and Tanya Chandrashekar.
  • Stage managers: Melvin Francis and Balu Chakravarthy.
  • Costumes: Krishna Kumar, Bangalore.

Program

  • Bharatanatyam Ranga Pravesha of Aadhya Sure and Thanvi Vashishta Nagineni.
  • Saturday, July 25th, 2026, from 3:00pm onwards at Shenkman Arts Centre.
  • Doors open at 2:30 PM so guests can be seated before the program begins.

Event Guidelines

  • Ages 5 and above are welcome.
  • Please arrive early and avoid entering or leaving during a dance piece.
  • Please keep phones on silent during the performance.
  • For any concerns, please contact Rammohan at 613-501-4918 or Ramana at 613-793-6116.
