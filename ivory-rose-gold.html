With hearts full of joy and gratitude, we invite you to witness Ananya’s Arangetram — the culmination of years of devotion to the art of Bharatanatyam. 03The Margam

The
Programme

From invocation to benediction, the traditional repertoire unfolds.

  1. 01PushpanjaliAn offering of flowers; invocation and salutation to the divine, the guru, and the audience.
  2. 02AlarippuThe blossoming; pure rhythmic movement that awakens the body and the stage.
  3. 03JatiswaramA lyrical interplay of rhythm and melody, danced in pure nritta.
  4. 04ShabdamThe first expressive piece; a devotional narrative in praise of the divine.
  5. 05VarnamThe centerpiece; an intricate weave of rhythm, story, and emotion at the heart of the margam.
  6. 06PadamTender abhinaya; the language of expression and devotion.
  7. 07ThillanaA sparkling, joyous finale of intricate rhythm and sculpturesque poses.
  8. 08MangalamA closing benediction; gratitude and blessings to all.
