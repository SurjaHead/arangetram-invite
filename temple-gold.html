The Invitation

With joy & gratitude

आङ्गिकं भुवनं यस्य वाचिकं सर्ववाङ्मयम् ।
आहार्यं चन्द्रतारादि तं वन्दे सात्त्विकं शिवम् ॥

Āṅgikaṃ bhuvanaṃ yasya vācikaṃ sarva-vāṅmayam
āhāryaṃ candra-tārādi taṃ vande sāttvikaṃ śivam

“We bow to Lord Shiva, the embodiment of grace — whose body is the universe, whose speech is all language, whose adornments are the moon and the stars.”

With hearts full of joy and gratitude, we invite you to witness Ananya’s Arangetram — the culmination of years of devotion to the art of Bharatanatyam. On this sacred day, she takes the stage alone for the first time, offering the fruits of her training to her gurus, her family, and the divine. Your presence and blessings would mean the world to us.

The Margam

The Programme

The traditional repertoire, unfolding from invocation to benediction.

  1. IPushpanjaliAn offering of flowers; invocation and salutation to the divine, the guru, and the audience.
  2. IIAlarippuThe blossoming; pure rhythmic movement that awakens the body and the stage.
  3. IIIJatiswaramA lyrical interplay of rhythm and melody, danced in pure nritta.
  4. IVShabdamThe first expressive piece; a devotional narrative in praise of the divine.
  5. VVarnamThe centerpiece; an intricate weave of rhythm, story, and emotion at the heart of the margam.
  6. VIPadamTender abhinaya; the language of expression and devotion.
  7. VIIThillanaA sparkling, joyous finale of intricate rhythm and sculpturesque poses.
  8. VIIIMangalamA closing benediction; gratitude and blessings to all.
